txtpod

Turn any article into your own podcast episode. txtpod saves links, scrapes the text, and narrates it with high fidelity, high quality AI voices in 20+ languages and 30+ styles. Each narration is delivered to your private podcast feed, ready to stream in Overcast, Pocket Casts, Apple Podcasts, and more.

txtpod focuses on podcast integration. You get a private feed, 30+ high quality voices, 20+ languages with auto translate, optional AI summaries, and customization. It fits right into your existing podcast habits instead of creating another reading app silo.
