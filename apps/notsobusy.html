NotSoBusy

NotSoBusy lets you instantly share your daily or weekly availability without exposing your full calendar. Just tap and send a clean, private view of your blocked time.

No logins, no accounts, and no data ever leaves your device. Perfect for quick plans and privacy-first sharing.
