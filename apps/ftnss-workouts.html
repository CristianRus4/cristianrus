ftnss Workouts

ftnss Workouts lets you design custom workouts for your Apple Watch, including warmups, cooldowns, and advanced interval training.

Seamlessly sync your custom workouts with Apple's built-in Workout app. No accounts, no ads, just powerful training tools for your wrist.

Privacy policy

Cristian Rus (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the ftnss Workouts app (the “Service”).

The service runs primarily on your device. We do not collect or store personal data ourselves. However, to provide AI-powered features, some anonymized data is sent to Google Gemini for processing. This data is stripped of identifiers and used solely to generate personalized workout suggestions and insights.

Purchase-related information is managed securely by RevenueCat and used only to verify and restore purchases if you reinstall the app.
