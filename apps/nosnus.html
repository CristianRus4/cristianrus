NoSnus

NoSnus helps you quit nicotine pouches for good with a personalised plan built just for you. Instead of a one-size-fits-all approach, it looks at your real consumption and daily logs to design a tapering path that fits your habits and moves at a pace you can actually stick to.

Every plan is grounded in science-proven methods for reducing nicotine dependence, gradually lowering your intake while keeping cravings manageable. Log each pouch, track your progress, and watch your dependence shrink day by day until you're finally free.
