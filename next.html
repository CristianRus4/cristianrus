Organize Your Life in One Place

Next is your comprehensive solution for managing daily tasks, events, health data, and more. Built with simplicity in mind, our app brings together your reminders, calendar events, health metrics, and weather information in a friendly, intuitive interface. Whether you're planning your day or tracking your wellness goals, Next provides all the tools you need in one beautiful app.

With seamless integration with Apple's ecosystem, Next acts as your central hub for personal organization. Access your reminders, check your calendar, monitor your health data, and stay updated with weather forecasts, all while maintaining your privacy. Plus, with built-in features like a Pomodoro timer, Next helps you stay focused and productive throughout your day.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2024

Introduction

Cristian Rus ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Next mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Data Collection and Use

We do not collect any information from our users. Next operates entirely on your device and syncs directly with your Apple services (Reminders, Calendar, Health, and Weather). Your data remains within Apple's ecosystem and is never transmitted to our servers.

Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Next operates as a client-side application, meaning all your data stays on your device or within Apple's secure ecosystem. We never have access to your personal information.
