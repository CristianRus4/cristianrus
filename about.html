Hi, I’m Cristian Rus, a digital designer based in Auckland, New Zealand.

For more than a decade, I have focused on how design and technologies influence society and human behaviour. The goal is to communicate and find solutions from a design perspective to how customers and businesses interact.

Previously I worked as a tech journalist at Webedia and as a designer for different companies, researching user interactions, creating interfaces and designing digital spaces.

The most effective way to contact me is by email. Otherwise, if we’re in the same city, we can have a coffee or go for a walk.
